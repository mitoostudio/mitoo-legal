## 1. Purpose
Mitoo is a visual time-perception and positive reinforcement system for families. Users select thematic scenes with ambient audio to intuitively grasp time through visual transformations. Upon completing a visual time session, an achievement badge is generated. Once manually collected, it is stored in the digital Badge Gallery. This digital collection serves as a visual record to support parents in structuring their own off-screen rewards. The App is a daily assistance system and does not replace professional behavioral, psychological, or medical advice.
